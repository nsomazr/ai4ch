AI4CropHealth

The AI4CropHealth project, based at the Department of Informatics and Information Technology, College of Natural and Applied Sciences, Sokoine University of Agriculture, is a groundbreaking initiative. Funded by the government of the United Republic of Tanzania through COSTECH, its mission is to transform crop disease surveillance, prediction, and management practices.
